ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Maria Cortez, I am a 23-year-old Graphic Designer from Lima, Peru. I studied for 5 years at the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences (UPC) and graduated
by the end of 2020 as a Bachelor in Graphic Design. Currently, I am working remotely as a part-time graphic designer at an agency in New York.

My hobbies are watching movies and tv shows, especially musical comedies and animated films from Disney. I also enjoy listening to different genres of music in order to increase
my Spotify playlist. I find relaxing riding my bike in the afternoons when is fresh, even though I am not a very sporty person.
